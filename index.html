Bio

Reggie Lawrence is a classical guitarist currently studying a masters in Musical Performance at The University of Hull. Outside of his studies Reggie freelances as a teacher and performer in the Yorkshire and Humberside regions, a feat he finds both challenging and rewarding. Reggie was first introduced to the guitar at age seven and hasn’t put one down since! After working through the musical grades and being awarded the first prize for classical guitar at the Robin Hood Music Festival 2010, Reggie embarked on an undergraduate in Music at The University of Hull, as well as launching his career as a guitarist. Outside of the guitar Reggie also plays the Piano, Theorbo and Digeridoo. His love of music does not stop at performing; he is an accomplished composer whom has been commissioned to write tracks for a freelance games development team. Reggie enjoys incorporating his own music into his own live music performances. Many exciting future projects lie ahead for Reggie, including collaborations with The University of Hull’s Film and Screen department as well a future tour with guitarist Richard Silvester following their successful series of concerts from last spring.
